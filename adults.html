Storytelling for Adults

I am currently the resident storyteller at Thimblemill Library, where I bring the best of storytelling to Bearwood in the Black Country. I have told stories to appreciative adult audiences in a variety of settings from cafes to church halls to private parties. Some of my stories are funny, some are thought-provoking, others are decidedly Grimm, but always they are enjoyable. I can offer several ready-made storytelling shows, all based on traditional and folk tales:

A Way Through the Woods

A mysterious lifetime’s journey told through interwoven traditional tales: man in harmony with the rhythm of the seasons in deep forests and on ancient roads.

It Was a Dark and Stormy Night

Prepare to be chilled by creepy tales of ghosts, ghouls and all sorts of supernatural happenings; perfect for when the nights draw in and the spooks come out.

Fiery Fables, Winter Tales

Be transported to frozen lands where miraculous legends are born. Hear some scorchingly good stories to warm you when the days are cold, and maybe meet a few winter spectres too.

High Seas, Dirty Deeds

An exuberant collection of stories of smugglers, storms and shipwrecks with, of course, a few pirates for good measure. There will be the occasional sea shanty and no buckle will be left unswashed.

Do contact me to discuss your needs; I have a large repertoire of stories and I can adapt to most occasions.
