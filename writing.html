Writing for Children

‘Literacy skills are crucial to pupils’ learning in other subjects across the curriculum’
Ofsted, Moving English Forward, 2012

As an experienced teacher and storyteller, I can bring a fresh, lively and invigorating style to creative writing workshops for children, offering exciting starting points to stimulate children to create their own original work.

My workshops include a variety of absorbing exercises which will help the children to develop some of the necessary skills of creative writing: writing plausible characters, dialogue, plot and description. Depending on the age of the participants, I may include reflection on point of view and how to draw in the reader.

The children will each have completed several pieces of short writing by the end of the workshop and will have had the encouragement and stimulus to continue writing.

I am a keen writer and I have taught creative writing to adults. My approach was to identify needs and interests within the group and to devise exercises accordingly, as well as maintaining a friendly supportive atmosphere where students felt safe to share their work. My creative writing class was rated ‘Excellent’ by Ofsted.

I am very experienced as a special educator and I am more than happy to bring creative writing and literacy workshops to special schools and settings. I specialise in children with SLD, PMLD and ASD.

I am a member of the National Association of Writers in Education.
